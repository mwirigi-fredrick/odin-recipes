All Fruits Smoothies

This classic recipe is prepared by Pouring pineapple juice into a blender and add banana, strawberries, and blueberries.

Cover and blend until smooth, about 1 minute. Pour into 2 glasses.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 large banana,cut into chunks
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Pour pineapple juice into a blender
  2. and add banana, strawberries, and blueberries
  3. Cover and blend until smooth, about 1 minute
  4. Pour into 2 glasses.
